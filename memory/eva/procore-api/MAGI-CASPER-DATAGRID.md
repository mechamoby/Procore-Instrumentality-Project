## COMPANY BACKGROUND

### 1. What is Datagrid?
**Founded:** November 2019  
**Location:** San Francisco Bay Area  
**Founder/CEO:** Thiago da Costa (joined Procore post-acquisition to lead AI & data strategy)

Datagrid is an agentic AI platform that connects fragmented enterprise systems and executes multi-step workflows autonomously. Unlike typical AI assistants that answer questions, Datagrid's agents "read docs, connect tools, and get the job done."
